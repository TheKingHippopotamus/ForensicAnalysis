## What These Reports Contain

Each report is a **complete forensic investigation** of a single company — built entirely from primary sources (SEC EDGAR filings and earnings call transcripts). No third-party data vendors. No consensus estimates. No recycled commentary. Just raw filings, rigorous analysis, and independent conclusions.

A typical report covers:

**Forensic Detection** — Six quantitative models scan financial statements for signs of earnings manipulation, distress risk, and accounting quality deterioration. These aren't screening tools — they're full multi-year computations with variable-level decomposition showing exactly *what* is driving each score and *why* it matters.

**Management Under Oath** — Earnings calls are the only time executives speak without a script. Our transcript analysis goes far beyond sentiment — it tracks specific promises across quarters, measures whether management delivered, detects linguistic patterns associated with evasion, and maps which topics top-tier analysts keep pressing on quarter after quarter.

**The Story vs The Numbers** — The most valuable findings come from cross-validation: what management *says* in the Q&A compared to what the XBRL data *actually shows*. When the CEO calls margins "resilient" but the filings show three consecutive quarters of compression — that contradiction is the signal.

**Governance & Incentive Alignment** — Proxy statement analysis reveals whether executives are compensated for creating shareholder value or for hitting easy targets. Board independence, related party transactions, entrenchment indicators — the structural factors that determine long-term capital stewardship.

**Institutional & Insider Flow** — Who's buying. Who's selling. Whether insiders are putting their own money where their mouth is. Whether sophisticated institutional investors are building or exiting positions — and the timing relative to material events.

## Methodology

Reports are produced by a proprietary autonomous research system that reads, reasons about, and cross-validates SEC filings at a depth that would take a human analyst weeks. The system doesn't generate summaries — it forms independent conclusions backed by specific evidence from primary sources.

Every finding in every report traces back to a specific filing, a specific data point, or a specific management statement — with confidence scoring that reflects how many independent sources corroborate the conclusion.

The analysis covers: earnings quality forensics, balance sheet integrity, cash flow dynamics, working capital signals, segment evolution, revenue recognition mechanics, debt structure, governance alignment, competitive positioning, supply chain dependencies, capital allocation patterns, regulatory exposure, management credibility scoring, and forward-looking predictions with explicit monitoring triggers.

**The underlying research system is private. The reports are free.**
